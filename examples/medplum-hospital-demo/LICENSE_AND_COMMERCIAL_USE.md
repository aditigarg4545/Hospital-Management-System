## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I remove the copyright headers?

**A:** No, you must keep them. But you can add your own alongside them.

### Q: Do I have to open-source my modifications?

**A:** No! Apache 2.0 allows closed-source modifications.

### Q: Can I sell this without paying Medplum?

**A:** Yes for the code. But if using Medplum cloud API, you pay for that service separately.

### Q: What if I use this in a proprietary product?

**A:** Totally allowed! Just include the license and copyright notices.

### Q: Can I rename and rebrand it?

**A:** Yes! Just keep the license notices somewhere.

### Q: Do I need to share my source code?

**A:** No, Apache 2.0 doesn't require source code sharing.

### Q: What about "SPDX" in the headers?

**A:** It's just a standard way to declare licenses. Keep it as-is.

### Q: Can I use in a SaaS product?

**A:** Yes! Many SaaS products use Apache 2.0 licensed code.

### Q: What about the Medplum trademark?

**A:** You can say "built with Medplum" but can't claim your product IS Medplum.
